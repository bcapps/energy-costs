Heat pumps

Big incentive. One modern system that heats and cools.

Heat pumps are the modern and efficient heating and cooling system for homes. Today's systems are built to handle New Jersey winters and can be less maintenance than other systems. And new for 2026, there's a huge incentive from PSE&G that lasts a limited time.

Why people are looking at heat pumps

  • Huge incentives from PSE&G starting in 2026.
  • Heating and cooling in one system means less maintenance.
  • Electric resistance and fuel oil customers can save big on heating bills.
  • Slimmer profiles and more configurable options mean ducts aren't a requirement.

PSE&G's program also includes standard HVAC instant rebates and larger decarbonization incentives for qualifying projects.
