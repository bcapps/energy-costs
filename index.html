Heat pumps and modern heating/cooling

Big incentive. Newer tech. One system that heats and cools.

Heat pumps are a serious option now, especially for homes using electric heat or delivered fuels like oil. Today's cold-climate systems are built to handle New Jersey winters.

Top rebate $12,000

For qualifying income-based cold-climate heat pump projects.

0% on-bill repayment $50,000

Available for eligible building decarbonization measures.

PSE&G's program also includes standard HVAC instant rebates and larger decarbonization incentives for qualifying projects.

Why people are looking at heat pumps

  • Heating and cooling in one system.
  • Modern cold-climate units are designed for winter performance.
  • Cleaner indoor comfort without combustion in the home.
  • Strong incentives can change the math on a major replacement.

EV savings

Lower the sticker shock. Get help with charging, too.

New Jersey and PSE&G both have programs that can trim the cost of an EV and make home charging easier to set up.

Charge Up New Jersey $1,500

Point-of-sale incentive on an eligible EV at a participating dealer.

Income-qualified offer $4,000

Total incentive when the extra Charge Up+ amount is added.

Home charger help $250

State incentive for a qualifying in-home Level 2 charger.

PSE&G make-ready help $1,500

Up to this amount per charger for customer-side electrical work.

  • Use the state program first to cut the upfront price of the vehicle.
  • Stack charger help if you need a Level 2 setup at home.
  • PSE&G may also reduce required customer deposits by up to $5,000 for some utility-side upgrade work.
